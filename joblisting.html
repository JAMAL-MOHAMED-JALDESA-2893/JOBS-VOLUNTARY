Agape Volunteers

Dreamt up by a team of international volunteers and local aid workers in 2009, Agape ensures that all its programmes have genuine benefits for all communities in need. Volunteers, like you select from a broad range of programmes, including the ones mentioned above, childcare, sports coaching, wildlife conservation and music teaching. On top of that, they also offer 'adventures', including safaris, mountain climbing and treks, to ensure that your service is not only memorable and life-changing to the locals, but also to you.

GoEco

Started by two lifelong friends, Jonathan Gilben and Jonathan Tal, who had an appetite for travelling the world and volunteering to help the locals, they started GoEco, a company which allowed them to link international volunteers to places in need, after obtaining approval from the Israeli Nature Park Authority. During its first year of operation, it focused on bringing volunteers to help with sea turtle conservation in the Mediterranean. Today, thousands of individuals are currently participating in over 150 volunteer projects around the world, and you can be a part of this amazing community as well! A dedicated team to assisting volunteers is available, incase of an emergency.

To join this NGO, please contact them on +16462404545 to claim your place!

VSO

Voluntary Service Overseas, or VSO for short, bring about change not by sending aid, but by working through volunteers and partners to empower communities in some of the world's poorest regions. They have experience working in over 90 countries, have worked with over 80,000 volunteers, and have supported over 50,000,000 people, and are currently adapting their work to respond to the most pressing needs(COVID-19). The organisation believes that progress is only possible when people work together thus, needs volunteers like you to assist them in a wide variety of jobs.

To participate in Voluntary Service Overseas, please contact them at +44 (0)20 8780 7500 to be a part of this growing organisation.
